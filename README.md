# Homework Helper
## Overview
We created a tool to help students manage assignments and their grades more effectively!  

https://github.com/jennyyn/CS-2520-final-project

## Features and their purpose
**1. Assignment Tracker -** Allows students to add, organize, and update assignments by due date, including marking them as "in progress", "done", or "not started"

**2. GPA Calculator -** Calculates a student's GPA based on course information, providing insight into academic performance

**3. What-If Assignment Calculator -** Lets students simulate hypothetical to seee how different grades would affect overall GPA

**4. Rewards System -** Visually represents assignment progress with plant images, giving students a fun way to track their progress

## How to Use Homework Helper

1. **Launch the Program**  
   Run `HomeworkHelperGUI.py` to open the main menu.

2. **Assignment Tracker**  
   - Click the **Assignment Tracker** button.  
   - Add assignments by providing the name, subject, due date, and status.  
   - Update assignments to “in progress” or “done” as you complete them by clicking on the assignment and the respective status buttons.  
   - Assignments are organized by due date.

3. **GPA Calculator**  
   - Click the **GPA Calculator** button.  
   - Enter your grades to calculate your current GPA quickly.

4. **What-If Assignment Calculator**  
   - Click the **What-If Tool** button.
   - Input a hypothetical assignment grade to see how it would affect the overall grade in a particular course.
   - Add a hypothetical assignment by providing the subject, name, points earned, and points possible.
   - Click "Calculate What-If Outcome" to predict the grade of the subject.

5. **Rewards System**  
   - Click the **Rewards** button.  
   - A new window opens showing plant images representing assignment progress:  
     - **Empty plant** = not started  
     - **Half-grown plant** = in progress  
     - **Fully grown plant** = completed (randomly chosen)  
   - Use the vertical scrollbar if there are many assignments.
